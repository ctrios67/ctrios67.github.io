About Me


I am a born and raised New Jersey native hailing from Elizabeth, NJ. I lived there most of my life until I finished college and took my first job that brought me to two very different cities and lifestyles. I graduated from the New Jersey Institute of Technology with a B.S. in Computer Engineering and a technical specification in Telecommunications.


I decided to major in Computer Engineering due to my most profound childhood memory. As a child, I was always fascinated by the trains used by NJ Transit and the MTA. The start of my interest in engineering comes from waiting for (what is now known as) the D train with my father, and asking him who drives the trains we always see. The answer is of course, an engineer. The title just stuck with me growing up. Once I saw I could combine my love for computers into a different discipline of engineering, my decision was solidified.


One of my favorite things to do is travel. I love to experience a city's customs, culture, food, and architecture. Since it is one of my favorite things to do, I always make it a mission to visit a city or town's transit system to see and hopefully snap a photo of their train.
