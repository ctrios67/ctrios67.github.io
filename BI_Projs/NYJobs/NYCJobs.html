This is a Tableau project I made that aims to answer the 4 following business questions:

  1. Which NYC Government Agency has the most job openings currently?
  2. Which Agency offers the lowest salary for a position?
  3. Which Agency offers the highest salary for a position?
  4. Which Agency is struggling to fill their positions?
